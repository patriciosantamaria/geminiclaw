# Vopak Corporate Style Guide (Branding v3.0)

Official standards for all documents, slides, and sheets generated by the Ultimate AI Assistant.

## 🎨 Visual Identity (Color Palette)
- **Vopak Deep Blue (Primary):** `#0a2373` (Use for headings, text, and backgrounds)
- **Vopak Cyan (Contrast):** `#00cfe1` (Use for emphasis and 'Flow Forward' arrows)
- **Secondary Colors:**
    - Light Blue: `#009ef5`
    - Cobalt: `#283ce1`
    - Green: `#52d400`
    - Orange: `#fc7000`
    - Steel: `#46555a`
    - Grey: `#e1e1e1`

## 🔤 Typography
- **Primary (Design/Slides):** Inter (Google Font)
- **Secondary (Docs/Sheets):** Arial (Standard Professional)
- **Weights:** Bold for headers, Regular for body.

## 📄 Document Structure (Docs)
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned body, Right-aligned project headers.
- **Tone:** Matter-of-fact, clear, and direct. Use "Trust, Collaboration, Courage" principles.
- **Writing:** Relevant, Simple, Human. Front-load important information.

## 📊 Spreadsheet Standards (Sheets)
- **Header:** Frozen Row 1, Deep Blue background (`#0a2373`), White text, Bold.
- **Borders:** Thin Steel Grey (`#46555a`) for grid lines.
- **Formatting:** Use Cyan (`#00cfe1`) for active 'Status' indicators.

## 📽️ Presentation Standards (Slides)
- **Layout:** One message per slide. Use 'Duo-imagery' or 'Flow Forward Arrows' for visual impact.
- **Arrows:** Forward arrow (Cyan) at the end of taglines or summary points.
- **Logo:** Diapositive (white) on dark backgrounds; Deep Blue on light backgrounds.
